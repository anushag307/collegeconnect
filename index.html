What We Do

Bootcamps and Hackathons

The J-Hub at JNTUH will conduct on regular basis the Hackathon events based on the Challenges posed by the Industry and Startups for the stakeholders in the form of a league events.

Accelerator

J-Hub proposes to launch from this academic year a 6 month accelerator program. The top teams of EXCITE 2017 shall become eligible to apply. The selection to the accelerator program shall be through a committee of industry experts.

Summer Product Engineering Workshop- EXCITE

EXCITE is a 6-Week summer product Engineering workshop being held annually at JNTUH.The students successfully built prototypes of their preliminary ideas registered at the beginning of the workshop.
